# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Buddy Review is a creator-first platform. About 80% of the experience is for influencers and creators who manage their creator identity, discover jobs, build portfolios, create rate cards, and receive personalized insights from mobile devices. About 20% is for brands and admins who review creators, manage campaigns, and support operations.

## Product Purpose

The product helps creators become more brand-ready and get hired. It should make creator progress visible, help users improve their profile, discover better-fit jobs, package their work into a shareable profile, and understand what to do next. Success means creators feel proud of their profile, motivated to level up, confident sharing their page with brands, and clear about their next action.

## Brand Personality

Empowering, modern, and playful professional. The closest emotional blend is LinkedIn plus Duolingo plus Spotify Wrapped for creators: credible enough for paid brand work, energetic enough to make progress feel rewarding, and polished enough to be screenshot-worthy.

## Anti-references

Do not make the product look like a generic SaaS purple dashboard with too many tables, KPI cards, or empty whitespace. Avoid corporate agency website cues that feel static, service-led, or PowerPoint-like. Avoid dark glassmorphism crypto dashboard patterns with heavy glow, blur, and low readability. Avoid childish creator-app treatment with excessive emojis, cartoon elements, or interruptive gamification popups.

## Design Principles

Mobile first: creators should be able to understand status, act, and share from a phone.

Shareable by default: major insights, profile summaries, and progress moments should feel polished enough to screenshot or send to a brand.

Progress is always visible: important surfaces should keep creator tier, XP progress, profile strength, and job match score close to the action.

Action-oriented: every screen should help the creator do something concrete, such as discover jobs, improve a profile, generate content ideas, increase tier, or get hired.

Premium creator economy, not agency dashboard: use Buddy Review purple, pink, white, and black with careful gradients, high readability, and creator confidence rather than generic SaaS styling.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and interactive controls. Respect reduced motion. Do not rely on color alone for progress or status. Keep mobile tap targets comfortable and avoid interactions that depend on hover only.
